Overview

CHRO+ offers medical-grade wearable devices designed to dramatically improve the efficiency of clinical trials. These devices support continuous monitoring for up to 7 days and enable free movement in natural environments.

  • Market Size (TAM): $184.5B (2030 Projection)
  • Growth Rate: CAGR of 19.5% (2023-2030)
  • Goal: Leadership in Decentralized Clinical Trials (DCT)

Products

Our wearable devices are equipped with the following features:

  • Continuous data recording for 7 days
  • Multimodal data acquisition: EEG, ECG, bio-impedance, etc.
  • Comfortable long-term wear
  • Designed for unrestricted movement

Services

CHRO+ provides comprehensive data collection and analysis services:

  • End-to-end solutions from data collection to analysis
  • Support for decentralized clinical trial design
  • Accurate data reporting
